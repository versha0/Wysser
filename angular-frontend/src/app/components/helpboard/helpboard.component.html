For Retailers

  1. If you are not registered go to the Signup Page , If you are already a member go to Login screen
  2. Fill in the required details and click on Register, On clicking on register you will recieve a confirmation mail, you can close the tab and go to your mail account
  3. You will recieve a mail which will have a link to set the new password for your account , you can set your password after you click that link
  4. After you have set your password, you will be automatically redirected to login page for the one time login
  5. Once logged in you will be sent to the edit profile section if you are a new member there you need to update your information
  6. After your profile is updated you can start using our renting services

To rent a vehicle


  1. Click on rent vehicle tab next to dashboard
  2. Fill in the required values as per your requirement and then click on search and then raise a request for booking a vehicle among the options
  3. Once a request is raised you can logout and you will be notified when your request is accepted
  4. Once booking is confirmed for the slot you can place you delivery by clicking on 'Add-order' tab next to 'Rent Vehicle'
  5. Once you place the order you will recieve a confirmation mail with details of the vehicle and pickup time
  6. Our representatives will collect the order from your doorstep 15 mins prior to the delivery slot

For Vehicle Company

  1. If you are not registered go to the Signup Page , If you are already a member go to Login screen
  2. Fill in the required details and click on Register, On clicking on register you will recieve a confirmation mail, you can close the tab and go to your mail account
  3. You will recieve a mail which will have a link to set the new password for your account , you can set your password after you click that link
  4. After you have set your password, you will be automatically redirected to login page for the one time login
  5. Once logged in you will be sent to the edit profile section if you are a new member there you need to update your information
  6. You can click on the car icon to switch between tabs
  7. After your profile is updated you can start accepting bookings from dashboard
  8. After you accept a request your secure driver password will be generated that you need to provide to your driver which he needs for login

Drivers

  1. Login using the vehicle number and the secure password provided to you by your company
  2. Once logged in you can see your delivery details
  3. Click on navigate to use the optimized maps provided by us
  4. Please do not cancel any deliveries unless its unavoidable
